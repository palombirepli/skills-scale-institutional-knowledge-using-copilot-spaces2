# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX / Design Lead

### Role Summary
The UX/Design Lead owns the user experience vision and design artifacts for the project. They bridge user research and product requirements with practical, implementable designs.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct or coordinate user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Maintain design system consistency across features
- Collaborate with Product Managers on acceptance criteria that include UX standards
- Partner with Developers to ensure accurate and accessible implementation

### Goals
- Deliver intuitive, accessible experiences that meet user needs
- Reduce rework caused by late-stage design changes
- Ensure design decisions are traceable and documented

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and feedback sessions with product and engineering
- Usability test findings shared with PdM and PM
- Design specs linked in backlog items and pull requests

---

## Tech Lead / Engineering Lead

### Role Summary
The Tech Lead provides overall technical direction, ensures architectural integrity, and removes complex technical blockers for the delivery team.

### Responsibilities
- Define and communicate technical architecture and key design decisions
- Conduct architecture and design reviews for significant changes
- Serve as escalation point for complex technical issues
- Contribute to estimates and capacity planning alongside the PM
- Champion engineering best practices (code quality, testing, observability)

### Goals
- Maintain a high-quality, maintainable, and scalable codebase
- Reduce technical debt and unplanned rework
- Enable the team to deliver predictably by resolving technical uncertainty early

### Typical Communication
- Technical design docs and ADRs (Architecture Decision Records)
- PR reviews and engineering syncs
- Risk and dependency inputs to PM during planning and execution

---

## Security / Compliance Officer

### Role Summary
The Security/Compliance Officer ensures that project deliverables meet applicable security standards, internal policies, and regulatory requirements throughout the lifecycle.

### Responsibilities
- Review architecture, code, and infrastructure for security vulnerabilities
- Own and maintain the security incident runbook
- Coordinate security-related escalations and post-incident reviews
- Validate security scans and controls as part of the pre-release checklist
- Advise on data privacy and compliance implications

### Goals
- Prevent security incidents through proactive review and risk mitigation
- Ensure compliance requirements are met without blocking delivery
- Maintain clear documentation of security decisions and findings

### Typical Communication
- Security review sign-off as part of release gate
- Incident communications and blameless retrospectives
- Regular updates to the Risk Register for security-related items

---

## Data / Analytics Engineer

### Role Summary
The Data/Analytics Engineer designs, builds, and maintains the data infrastructure needed to track project success metrics and inform product decisions.

### Responsibilities
- Build and maintain data pipelines, dashboards, and reporting tooling
- Partner with Product Managers to define and validate KPIs
- Implement instrumentation and observability hooks with Developers
- Provide data-driven insights to support retrospectives and planning

### Goals
- Ensure metrics are reliable, timely, and actionable
- Reduce time-to-insight for product and project decisions
- Enable a data-informed culture across the team

### Typical Communication
- Dashboard and metric readouts in weekly syncs and retrospectives
- Collaboration with Developers on logging and event tracking
- Alignment with PdM on success metric definitions

---

## Customer Success / Support Representative

### Role Summary
The Customer Success/Support Representative brings the customer perspective into the project team, ensuring that user feedback and frontline support insights inform delivery priorities.

### Responsibilities
- Gather and synthesize user feedback and support trends
- Represent customer needs in backlog grooming and planning discussions
- Coordinate with PM on release communication plans and known issues documentation
- Participate in post-release reviews to identify recurring issues or unmet needs

### Goals
- Reduce customer-impacting issues through proactive input to the delivery process
- Improve the quality of release notes and stakeholder communications
- Shorten feedback loops between users and the product team

### Typical Communication
- Backlog grooming and sprint reviews
- Release readiness reviews and known-issues documentation
- Post-release support summaries shared with PM and PdM
